Club Leader — Active Role

The Athenaeum Club at JNTUK is a peer-driven skill exchange community where students teach what they know and learn what they don't. The philosophy is simple — every person holds unique knowledge worth sharing.

As leader, I drive the club's vision: building a culture where curiosity meets collaboration, and where skills in design, development, cybersecurity, and beyond flow freely between members.
